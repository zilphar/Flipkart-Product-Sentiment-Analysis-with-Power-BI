# Flipkart-Product-Sentiment-Analysis-with-Power-BI

**<ins> Introduction </ins>** 

Flipkart is a major online store that deals with products including laptops. The Category managers in the electronics side want to know the number and different types of laptops sold, and the sentiment analysis for each laptop sold. This information will helpful to them understand customer feedbakc on specific laptop brands which in turn helps them optimize product listing based on customer preferences. 

**<ins> Questions to Answer</ins>**

1. How are the reviews for Laptops sold from the store? 

**<ins> Insights Derived </ins>** 

A total of 14 laptops were sold in the store with an average score rate of 4.46 and a total of 3300 reviews.

1. Most reviews for Laptops sold in the store are positive (2879 out of 3300 reviews) with most of them having a 5 (Outstanding) and 4 (Very satisfactory) rating. This means that the store is selling good quality products and that the customer expectations are highly being met. However a significant number of reviews are neutral and negative indicating room for improvement.
2. Of the 14 laptops reviewed, the Apple 2020 Macbook Air M1 had the most total reviews with the positive reviews leading for all other laptops. The HP Pavilion Windows 10 comes second, however it has the most negative reviews at 79 of the total 341 negative reviews. These two also lead in terms of the total sale price. This most likely indicates the high demand for such products driven by customer satisfaction. These two flagships are highly desirable due to their reliability and performance.

The negative reviews in the HP laptop suggest that there may be concerns about the products quality. Overtime Hp might need to address the issues raised by these netagive reviews, since demand can start to decline as potential customers are influenced by negative feedback.
